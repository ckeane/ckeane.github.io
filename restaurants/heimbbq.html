The Food

Everything tasted great. I don't think there was a thing I didn't enjoy. The only possible complaint was that the turkey was a LITTLE bit dry (but still tasted great). My favorite part of the meal was the famous Bacon Burnt Ends. I think they make their BBQ sauce with molasses, because it was pretty damn sweet (and tasty).
